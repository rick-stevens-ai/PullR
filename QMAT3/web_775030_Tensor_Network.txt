**Summary:**  
This resource page provides a curated list of review articles, tutorials, lecture notes, and original research papers on tensor networks, a mathematical framework central to modern quantum many-body physics and related fields. The materials range from beginner-friendly introductions and practical coding guides to advanced technical reviews and seminal papers on key algorithms and theoretical developments. The collection serves as a comprehensive starting point for researchers and students seeking to understand or apply tensor network methods in physics, mathematics, and data science.
