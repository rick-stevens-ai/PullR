Abstract:
**Topic:** Quantum Information Science & Technology at Brookhaven National Laboratory (BNL)

**Key Points:**
- The Quantum Computing Group at BNL focuses on quantum algorithms, error correction, entanglement, and AI applications in quantum hardware/software.
- Research projects include hybrid digital-analog quantum algorithms, quantum algorithms across computational models, novel quantum primitives, quantum telescopes, and spin chain bootstrapping for quantum computation.
- The group collaborates with various physics and instrumentation departments and is part of the Co-design Center for Quantum Advantage (C2QA).

**Methods:** Research in quantum algorithms, theoretical and computational modeling, development of quantum protocols, and application of quantum information science to experimental physics.

**Authors:** Not specified (Group-based research at Brookhaven National Laboratory)
