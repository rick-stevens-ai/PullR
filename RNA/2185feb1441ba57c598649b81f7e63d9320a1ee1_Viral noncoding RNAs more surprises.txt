Title: Viral noncoding RNAs: more surprises
Authors: K. T. Tycowski, Y. Guo, Nara Lee, Walter N. Moss, Tenaya K Vallery et al. (and 2 more)
Year: 2015
Paper ID: 2185feb1441ba57c598649b81f7e63d9320a1ee1
URL: https://www.semanticscholar.org/paper/2185feb1441ba57c598649b81f7e63d9320a1ee1
Venue: Genes & Development

Abstract:
Many viruses make noncoding (nc)RNAs and the challenge is to define their functions. In this review, Tycowski et al. summarize what has been learned so far about the origin and function of virus-encoded ncRNAs.
